How it works

1

Post your request

Write a short title, add details and an optional photo, and pick the date you prefer.

2

Neighbors respond

Nearby helpers see your request and message you if they can help. You choose who to work with.

3

Agree & get it done

Coordinate time and price directly with the helper. When done, mark it complete. No platform fees.
