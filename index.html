▾ How it works
  1. Post your request with the task, date, and (optionally) a photo.
  2. Route we notify nearby helpers instantly by email.
  3. Agree on time & details directly with a helper. Mark it done when completed.

The platform is free — we don’t take any cuts. If you’d like to support the project, you can leave a small donation.
